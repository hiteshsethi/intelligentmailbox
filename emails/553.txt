<h2 align="center"><u>KSHITIJ 2012</u></h2>
<p>
"Knowledge must come through action; you can have no test which is not fanciful, save by trial."<br>
Constituted in 2004, Kshitij, the annual techno-management symposium of IIT Kharagpur, provides a platform for students to showcase and test their knowledge through its application in the fields of technology and management. It is an attempt to bring out the creativity lurking within every student.<br>
Scheduled from 27th to 30th of January, 2012, the ninth edition of Kshitij is all set to transcend its previous records.<br><br>

Already creating ripples across the nation are the series of guest lectures which include critically acclaimed actor <b>Nasseruddin Shah</b>, Acclaimed Journalist <b>Prabhu Chawla</b>, Inventor of USB <b>Ajay Bhatt</b>, NASA astronaut <b>Paulo Nespoli</b>, Father of Microprocessors <b>Federico Faggin</b>, Adventure Journalist <b>Robert Pelton</b>, Father of String Theory <b>Dr Holger Neilson</b>, Founder of Semantic Web <b>James Hendler</b>, Nobel Laureate <b>Sir James Mirrlees</b> and Director of Sub Atomic Particle Division, CERN <b>Dr Antonio Ereditato</b>. Detailed information can be found at <b>http://www.ktj.in/#guests.</b><br>
<b>Video Link:-</b> http://vimeo.com/35068733 <br><br>

The symposium also provides participants a wholesome opportunity to polish their technical prowess through several interactive workshops like <b>Cloud Computing</b> and <b>Enterprise Computing</b> by IBM, <b>Bomb Disposal</b> by CID Kolkata, <b>Epigraphical Studies</b> by Archaeological Survey of India, <b>Green Electronics</b> by Texas Instruments, <b>Alternative Construction and Disaster Response</b> by Tsunami Relief Foundation, India, <b>Indian Capital Markets</b> by NSE Kolkata and <b>Digital Forensics</b> by Cyber Society of India. Registrations for the same can be done at <b>http://www.ktj.in/#workshops.</b><br>
<b>Video Link:-</b> http://vimeo.com/35154249 <br><br>

Witness the magnanimity as it unfolds during the four days of the symposium. Participants please take note that the last date for the registration for accommodation is January 21, 2012. The accommodation form for the participants is now available on the website at http://www.ktj.in/#acco.<br>


The details for filling the accommodation form is as follows:
</p><b>
<li>Participants will have to make and bring along with them a draft of Rs. 1000/-(INR 200 is as a caution money for all the accommodation materials provided 
which, will be refunded after the fest) in favour of "Kshitij", payable at SBI, IIT Kharagpur (0202).
<li>If you are coming in a team (say 5 membered team), a single draft can be drawn for the whole amount (5 x 1000 = Rs. 5000). However, all team members are required 
to fill the form separately. If they have a common DD then the same DD number is to be entered in all the forms.
<li>Registrations for Accommodation will end on January 21st.
<li>Accommodation will be provided in a first come, first serve basis. Participants are required to update their accommodation forms with the PNR number of their

2)Accommodation Form Printout<br> 
3)Demand Draft<br>
