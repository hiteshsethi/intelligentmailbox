<h2 align="center"><u>ACCOMMODATION DETAILS</u></h2>
<p>
Kshitij is the annual Techno-management fest of IIT Kharagpur. This year it will be organized from 27th-30th January. The accommodation form for the participants is 
now available on the website at  http://www.ktj.in/#acco.<br><br>
The details for filling the accommodation form is as follows:
</p><b>
<li>Participants will have to make and bring along with them a draft of Rs. 1000/-(INR 200 is as a caution money for all the accommodation materials provided 
which, will be refunded after the fest) in favour of "Kshitij", payable at SBI, IIT Kharagpur (0202).
<li>If you are coming in a team (say 5 membered team), a single draft can be drawn for the whole amount (5 x 1000 = Rs. 5000). However, all team members are required 
to fill the form separately. If they have a common DD then the same DD number is to be entered in all the forms.
<li>Registrations for Accommodation will end on January 21st.
<li>Accommodation will be provided in a first come, first serve basis. Participants are required to update their accommodation forms with the PNR number of their 
travelling ticket. 
<li>The following items must be brought by the participants during the registration at IIT Kharagpur:<br>
1)College Identity Card<br>
2)Accommodation Form Printout<br> 
3)Demand Draft<br> 
<li>All the finalists must register for Accommodation. They will be granted Accommodation as soon as they register. </b><br><br>
