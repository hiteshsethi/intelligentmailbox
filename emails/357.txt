<h3><strong><u>Marca De Fabrica</u><strong></h3 >
<p align="justified">The Swoosh; Just Do It; the half bitten Apple; the red colour on any 'Coca Cola' script, the 'Three Stripes', 'Have a break'; doesn't the very mention ring a bell? Things as fundamental as a name, a logo, a slogan, the type of script, the colour have the potential to make or break an enterprise. A great brand raises the bar -- it adds a greater sense of purpose to the experience, the affirmation that the cup of coffee you're drinking 
really matters. Branding has become a vital part of marketing any product.</p>
<p>Marca De Fabrica offers you the opportunity to market an idea and create its brand value, as you design an entire campaign for its advertising and marketing.</p>
<p>Prize Money for the event is INR 21,000. For more details visit: http://www.ktj.in/#Marca_De_Fabrica.</p><br/>

 

<h3 ><strong><u>Synergy </u><strong></h3> 
 <p>Ever reasoned why Microsoft and Yahoo didn't settle but Tata and Corus joined hands even when both of them would profit? Put yourself in the shoes of the CEO of your firm. The new edition of the most awaited Techno-management fest, Kshitij brings you Synergy, based on Mergers and Acquisitions in the corporate world.</p><p> Few companies of different genre and their annual report will be provided out of which you have to reason your decision of merging your 
 firm with the listed ones or acquiring them. You will have to research about your assets, liabilities and propose decisions to lead your company to the pinnacle.</p>
